WHAT IS NEXUS POWER

BUILT FOR PRECISION

Nexus Power fuses embedded engineering with modern web technology to give you full visibility and control over your power bank.

Real-Time BLE Monitoring

Live battery percentage, power draw, and charging state streamed wirelessly at one-second intervals directly to your phone.

Accurate Battery Tracking

Non-linear discharge curve with interpolation gives you a precise percentage reading across the full battery range — no more misleading numbers.

Cell Protection

Automatic output cutoff at 0% prevents deep discharge and permanent cell damage, so your battery lasts longer over time.

Progressive Web App

Install the dashboard on your Android or iPhone home screen. Works offline and loads instantly — no App Store required.

Hardware Authentication

Each unit carries a unique cryptographic identity. Unregistered or counterfeit hardware is detected and locked out automatically.

Remote Output Control

Toggle power output on or off from the dashboard with a single tap. State is preserved and restored automatically after every reboot.

HOW IT WORKS

THREE STEPS

01

POWER UP

Turn on the Nexus Power device. It boots instantly, restores the last power state, and begins wireless advertising.

02

CONNECT

Open the dashboard and tap Connect. Your phone pairs wirelessly — hardware identity is verified in the background.

03

MONITOR

Live battery level, power draw, and charging state update every second. Toggle the output with a single tap.
